Saturday Chicken Stock

This is not a quick recipie, but the results are worth it...

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 rotisserie chicken carcasses, broken into pieces
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. cook for 20 min
  3. let cool
  4. ENJOY!

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:

31 cals: 1g fat: 0mg cholesterol; 28mg sodium. 4g carb; .5g protein
